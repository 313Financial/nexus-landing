FAQ

Common questions.

What makes Nexus different from HubSpot or Salesforce?+
Nexus is built specifically for commercial finance — not adapted from a generic CRM. It understands BTL, bridging, development finance, DIP stages, LTV and lender workflows out of the box. You don't need to configure anything — it works the way you already work.
Does it integrate with Twenty7Tec?+
Yes. Push clients and deals from Nexus directly into Twenty7Tec as cases — no re-keying between systems. Requires API access from your T7T account manager.
How does the Companies House integration work?+
When a client submits their intake form with their limited company name, Nexus automatically queries the Companies House API and pulls their registered address, company number, incorporation date, SIC codes, directors list and company status — all pre-populated into their client record before you even open it.
Can multiple advisers use it, and can I see their activity?+
Yes. The Team plan supports up to 5 advisers. As the principal, you get a master broker view — you can see all clients, deals, tasks and activity across your whole firm, or switch to view as any individual adviser. Each adviser only sees their own data.
Is my data secure?+
Yes. Nexus is hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure with row-level security — each firm's data is completely isolated from every other firm's. All data is stored in UK/EU data centres and encrypted at rest and in transit.
