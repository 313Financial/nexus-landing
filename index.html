Features

Everything a commercial broker
actually needs.

No bloat. No generic sales CRM retrofitted for finance. Built from scratch for bridging, development and commercial mortgage brokers.

Commercial Finance Pipeline

Six stages built for how commercial finance actually works: Enquiry, DIP, Packaged, Submitted, Offered, Completed. Not adapted from sales — designed from scratch.

Automated Client Intake

Submit a client through a branded form. Company data is verified and enriched automatically. The full record appears in your CRM — without a single manual entry.

Instant Notifications

The moment a client is submitted, an alert lands on your phone with their full details. No logging in. No checking email. You know instantly.

Notes & Activity Log

Log calls, emails and meetings against every client record. Tagged by type, timestamped, permanent. Never lose context on a deal — even six months later.

Follow-up Engine

Set next action dates on every client. Overdue follow-ups surface on your dashboard automatically. Nothing slips. Nothing gets forgotten.

Team & Role Access

Master broker sees everything firm-wide. Advisers see only their own pipeline and clients. Role-based access that works how a brokerage actually operates.

How it works

From first contact to active client
in under 30 seconds.

01
Client submits your intake form
A branded online form captures contact details, company name, finance type and loan requirements.
02
Data is enriched automatically
Company information is verified and enriched in the background. No manual lookups. No copy-pasting.
03
Full record created instantly
A complete client record is written to your CRM with all enriched data attached. Permanently stored.
04
You're notified immediately
An instant alert lands on your phone with the full client summary. You know the moment it arrives.
05
Ready to work the deal
The client is in your pipeline with all data pre-filled. Add notes, set a follow-up date, move it forward.
From enquiry to active CRM record — enriched, notified and ready to progress — in under 30 seconds. No manual entry required.
